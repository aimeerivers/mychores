Quick-create format

Task - recurrence - due date
(recurrence and due date are optional)

Recurrence options (examples):
one-off
every day
every week
every month
every 3 days
every 6 weeks
every 4 months

Due date, if supplied, must be in the format YYYY-MM-DD or use the keyword tomorrow.

If you don't specify a recurrence your task template will be used (see Preferences).
If you don't specify a due date it will occur today.
